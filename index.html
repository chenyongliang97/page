I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Weitao Xu and Prof. Qingchuan Zhao. My primary research interests are on vulnerability discoveries with advancing techniques (program analysis, AI, and LLMs) and smart sensing. I obtained my B.E. degree in School of Computer Science and Engineering from South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020.
